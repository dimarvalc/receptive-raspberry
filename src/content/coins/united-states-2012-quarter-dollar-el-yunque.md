### Numismatic Historical Log
Part of the America the Beautiful Quarters series, this coin honors **El Yunque National Forest** in Puerto Rico. The reverse landscape view beautifully captures a Puerto Rican parrot (Cotorra Puertorriqueña) and a Coquí tree frog sitting amongst lush tropical flora.
