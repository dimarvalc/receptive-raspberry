### Technical Restoration Log
This advanced digital reconstruction project targets a heavily degraded, low-resolution portrait capture. The physical source asset suffered from catastrophic compression artifacting, severe chromatic color bleeding, and a complete loss of sharp focal data that flattened the subject's anatomical features and blurred complex hair and fabric textures into blocky pixel clusters.

#### Key Enhancements Completed:
* **Facial Geometry & Feature Restoration**: Reconstructed lost anatomical focal lines within the eyes, eyelids, and brows, transforming pixelated digital noise into a sharp, expressive human portrait.
* **Micro-Texture Hair Refinement**: Re-engineered the subject's distinct afro hair structure from a flat silhouette into an intricate field of individual micro-curls, restoring organic volume and lifelike shadow depth.
* **Dermal Artifact Mitigation**: Smoothed out harsh compression blocks and unnatural color bleeds across the skin tones, preserving natural structural lines and gentle facial contours.
* **Garment & Canvas Purification**: Sharpened the distorted edges of the blue polo shirt to bring out fabric weave textures, while swapping out a noisy, stained background for a pristine, professional studio-gray canvas.

### Historical Context & Technical Note
This project represents the cutting edge of digital preservation workflow, moving beyond simple tear repair into structural pixel reconstruction. By analyzing underlying facial anatomy and texture behaviors, the restoration successfully bridges the gap between old low-fidelity digital imaging limitations and modern high-resolution display standards, preserving a treasured portrait for future generations.
