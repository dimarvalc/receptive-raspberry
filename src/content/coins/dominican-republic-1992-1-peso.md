### Numismatic Historical Log

#### Physical & Strike Assessment
This specimen is a highly eye-catching circulation-strike commemorative featuring a multi-faceted 11-sided (hendecagonal) inner rim border. The obverse displays the central Dominican National Coat of Arms with the small open Bible text intact, balanced alongside the crisp, bold face value `1 PESO`. The reverse shows a frost-finished portrait of Christopher Columbus looking left. The coin exhibits strong cameo-like field contrast with sharp, mirrored background planes and minimal friction marking.

#### Historical Context & Lineage
Issued in 1992 to mark the historic milestone of the 500th anniversary of the Discovery and Evangelization of America (`V CENTENARIO DEL DESCUBRIMIENTO Y EVANGELIZACION DE AMERICA`). This specific sub-series design served a dual role—acting as an accessible, high-relief circulating commemorative for citizens while paying homage to Santo Domingo’s status as the initial focal point of European contact in the Caribbean basin. It remains a visually unique addition to regional 20th-century collection databases.
