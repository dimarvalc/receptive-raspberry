### Numismatic Historical Log

#### Physical & Strike Assessment
This pristine specimen features a spectacular Brilliant Uncirculated satin strike from the Philadelphia Mint. The obverse showcases John Flanagan’s classic George Washington profile, showing sharp frost details on the hair lines next to a crisp `P` mint mark. The reverse design features the endemic Coquí frog resting on a leaf in the lower left quadrant, while a Puerto Rican Parrot sits perched on a tree branch above. The fields are entirely clear of bag marks with a uniform, luminous mint luster.

#### Historical Context & Lineage
Released on January 23, 2012, this coin marks the 11th release within the United States Mint's America the Beautiful Quarters Program. This beautiful reverse composition uniquely honors Puerto Rico’s tropical rainforest ecosystem, showcasing two of its most famous endangered and native wildlife species. Added as a secondary duplicate sequence entry into the collection repository, this specific specimen was selected for its exceptional reverse die execution and crisp central focal point details.
