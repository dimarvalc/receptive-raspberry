### The Historical Context & Story
For the APEX 2026 show in Anchorage, Alaska, I wanted to capture the rugged spirit of polar postal history. This cover commemorates the opening day of the philatelic exhibition.

### The Gemini AI Design Journey
I collaborated with Gemini to develop a clean, high-contrast illustration style that would print sharply on the envelope surface without bleed. 

**The Design Prompt Shared with Gemini:**
> "Create a clean, minimalist line-art illustration of an Alaskan mountain range with sharp geometric vector lines, transparent alpha channel background, high-contrast design optimized for envelope printing."

### Production Notes
Printed successfully using my inkjet setup. I added the logo for the Anchorage Philatelic Society and the map of Anchorage, Alaska to the right of the design.
