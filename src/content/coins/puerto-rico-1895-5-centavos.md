### Numismatic Historical Log
This silver 5 Centavos piece was minted under Spanish colonial administration for circulation within Puerto Rico. The obverse features the youthful portrait profile engraving of King Alfonso XIII, while the reverse showcases the bold face value numeral within a clean leafy wreath matrix border.
